B2 is a hard disk music player that can store your entire CD collection. B2 is simple - you can use it like a CD player - just insert a CD and press Play.

THE BRENNAN / ABOUT

It takes just one button click and a few minutes to load (or rip) the CD onto the hard disk. Track, Album and Artist names are assigned from the internet as you load so you can find music by name. You don't need an internet connection though - B2 has an internal database of 3.5 million CDs.

Declutter your living room, get instant access to any track and play unbroken music at the touch of a button. B2 uses FLAC compression that's a lossless compression technique so B2 stores an exact copy of what is on the CD. 500Gb model can store 1100 CDs as FLAC.

Perfect unadulterated copies of your CDs forever
